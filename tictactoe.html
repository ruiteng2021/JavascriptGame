Tic Tac Toe

The game is for two players. One player is yellow(X), the other is orange(O). The goal is to get three boxes in a row(across, down, or diagonally). When you enter your letter in to a box and click OK, the box willchange your color. The game ends either when one player wins or there is a tie.
